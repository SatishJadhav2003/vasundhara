About the theme

The Vasundhara Sanstha have a unique capability to convene and converge partners to tackle systemic weaknesses in the education system in the country. The Trusts are working to bring about systemic change by engaging with state and central authorities to transform the educational system from within.

The Trusts’ interventions in this fields comprise building capacity of anganwadis, developing processes and frameworks to standardise learning, setting up school development indices that serve as feedback mechanisms for educators, etc. These programmes and projects under the Trusts’ education portfolio address a multitude of needs and challenges across the sector.

National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE)

The Vasundhara Sanstha were approached by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) to develop a model curriculum for teacher education. NCTE’s four-year integrated teacher education programme was designed to prepare pre-service teachers with the requisite knowledge, attitude, and skills to become professional teachers.

The goal is to develop a curriculum that incorporated the world’s best practices in the field of teacher education.

The Trusts’ support for the design of a new integrated curriculum is intended to bridge the gap between the theory and practice of teaching through:

  • Deeper engagement with the learner
  • Integration of content and pedagogy
  • Innovation of assessment practices
