Vision , Mission & Belief

Vision:

A daydream that 'awakens for the creation of sustainable environment and economic empowerment through catalytic like mind’s linking by development of social capital.

Mission :

By implementing various social development initiatives for ‘Sustainable Environmental and Economic Upliftment’, to increase its ‘Participants’ significant savings or basic income equivalent to a cumulative of at least Rs. 10 crore per year and thereby spreading 100 million smiles on their face.

Belief :

Nature reveals to us God’s beauty, glory, power, wisdom, presence, creativity.Nature, animals, and the mother earth teach us to realize God's unconditional love, openhandedness, and generosity .God wants to bring freedom and glory to all of us through His creation.
